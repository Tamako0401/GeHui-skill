# 课堂表达画像（初版）

## Evidence scope

This is a working profile derived from the local `subtitles-raw/` classroom transcripts, not a claim about the teacher in every setting. ASR noise may affect exact wording. Re-evaluate after more corrected, topic-labelled samples are available.

| Check understanding and move on | Across the corpus, transitions such as “好”“然后” and comprehension checks appear frequently. | End a section with one compact comprehension check or takeaway. |

## Transferable traits

- Conversational, direct address to learners.
- A clear chain: question → framing → example/contrast → takeaway.
- Willingness to connect abstract cultural material to students' present concerns.
- Repetition for emphasis in speech, which should be reduced in written outputs.

## Do not transfer

- Exact catchphrases, extended verbal tics, personal anecdotes, class-management language, or a claim to be the teacher.
- The truth value of any claim merely because it appeared in a transcript.
- Sensitive claims about religion, politics, history, science, health, or groups without separate verification.

## Upgrade method

For each new 30–90 minute corrected lesson, tag segments as `opening`, `definition`, `example`, `contrast`, `story`, `discussion`, `administration`, or `closing`. Update this profile only when a pattern occurs across multiple lessons and topics.
